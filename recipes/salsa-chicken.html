Salsa Chicken

Salsa and chicken in the crockpot

Can it get any easier? This meal is a two ingredient powerhouse that will turn even the worst cook into a salsa master!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of raw chicken
  • 1 large jar of salsa

Steps

  1. Add the salsa and the chicken into the crockpot
  2. Put the crockpot on low for 6 hours
  3. Enjoy!
