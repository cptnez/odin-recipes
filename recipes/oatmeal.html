Oatmeal with blueberries

The fabled oatmeal can now be made in the comfort of you own home! This recipe will make even the most stubborn child jump with glee each time you say"Oats for breakfast!".

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Old fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 teaspoons of brown sugar
  • cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup of blueberries

Steps

  1. Add the oats and water into a bowl
  2. Put the bowl in a microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds
  3. When the time is up, add the brown sugar, cinnamon, and blueberries
  4. Enjoy!
